# 🎯 TOPPER STRATEGY SUMMARY (Common Themes)

### 1. NCERT First, NCERT Always
Every topper emphasized: finish NCERT completely (exercises, examples, in-text questions) before touching any other book.

### 2. PYQs Are Your Best Friend
All toppers solved 5-10 years of previous year papers. Helps understand:
- Question patterns and weightage
- Answer writing style expected by CBSE
- Time management

### 3. 4-5 Revisions Minimum
The topper gold standard: revise each subject at least 4 times before exam day.

### 4. Mock Tests Under Exam Conditions
Take 10+ full-length timed mocks. Simulate real exam conditions.

### 5. Handwritten Notes Win
Make your own notes — toppers credit this as a key differentiator.

### 6. Daily Schedule (From Toppers)
- **School days**: 4-5 hours self-study
- **Exam season**: 8-10 hours
- **Always include**: 1 hour physical activity/outdoor games
- **Breaks**: 10-15 min between sessions

### 7. Digital Discipline
- Delete/deactivate Instagram during exam prep
- Use YouTube ONLY for study content
- Enable parental controls / screen time limits

### 8. Subject Strategy
- **Maths**: Daily practice of numericals, RD Sharma for extra
- **Science**: Understand concepts → NCERT → PYQs → Diagrams practice
- **SST**: Timelines, mind maps, structured answer writing with bullet points
- **English**: Daily reading, grammar practice, writing formats
- **Hindi/Sanskrit**: Grammar + writing practice + NCERT reading
